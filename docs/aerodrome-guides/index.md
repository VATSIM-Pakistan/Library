# Aerodrome Guides

Aerodrome guides provide operational guidance and recommended VATSIM procedures for major Pakistani airports. These guides are intended to help both pilots and controllers understand local operations, preferred runway usage, taxi procedures, SID usage, VFR operations, and general aerodrome practices commonly used within the VATSIM Pakistan FIR.

## Available Guides

### Southern Pakistan
- [Karachi / OPKC](opkc.md)  
  Jinnah International Airport guide covering runway operations, taxi procedures, RNAV departures, VFR traffic handling, and local ATC practices.

### Central Pakistan
- [Lahore / OPLA](opla.md)  
  Allama Iqbal International Airport guide including preferred runway operations, low visibility procedures, circuit operations, and departure handling.

### Northern Pakistan
- [Islamabad / OPIS](opis.md)  
  Islamabad International Airport guide covering CAT III operations, terrain-sensitive procedures, VFR circuit operations, and standard IFR departures.

---

!!! info
    These aerodrome guides are designed exclusively for flight simulation use on the VATSIM network and must not be used for real-world aviation or navigation purposes.

!!! tip
    Pilots are encouraged to review the relevant aerodrome guide before connecting to VATSIM Pakistan to ensure familiarity with local procedures and ATC expectations.

!!! warning
    Controllers may deviate from procedures outlined in these guides due to traffic, weather, events, or operational requirements.
