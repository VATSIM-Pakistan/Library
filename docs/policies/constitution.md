# Constitution Policy

## Overview

This document establishes the structure, governance, and operational framework of VATSIM Pakistan (vACC). It defines how the division is managed, how decisions are made, and how responsibilities are distributed among staff.

It ensures transparency, accountability, and consistency across all operations within VATSIM Pakistan. :contentReference[oaicite:0]{index=0}

---

## Purpose

The purpose of this policy is to:

- Define organizational structure  
- Establish roles and responsibilities  
- Provide governance guidelines  
- Ensure consistent decision-making  
- Maintain alignment with VATSIM global policies  

---

## Structure of VATSIM Pakistan

VATSIM Pakistan operates as a subdivision (vACC) under the VATSIM network.

### Leadership

The division is managed by appointed staff members responsible for operations, training, and administration.

Typical roles include:

- Division Director  
- Operations Director  
- Training Director  
- Web / Technical Team  
- Events & Community Team  

---

## Responsibilities

### Staff Responsibilities

Staff members are responsible for:

- Managing ATC operations  
- Conducting training and evaluations  
- Maintaining rosters  
- Handling feedback and support  
- Ensuring policy compliance  

---

### Member Responsibilities

All members are expected to:

- Follow VATSIM Code of Conduct  
- Follow VATSIM Code of Regulations  
- Follow Pakistan vACC policies  
- Maintain professional behavior  

Global VATSIM policies always take precedence where applicable. :contentReference[oaicite:1]{index=1}

---

## Decision Making

- Decisions are made by designated staff members  
- Major decisions may involve consultation with regional/divisional authorities  
- Policies are reviewed periodically and updated as required  

---

## Policy Compliance

All members and staff must comply with:

- VATSIM global policies  
- Pakistan vACC policies  
- Local procedures  

Failure to comply may result in disciplinary action.

---

## Enforcement

Violations may result in:

- Warnings  
- Removal from roster  
- Suspension of privileges  
- Escalation to VATSIM authorities  

---

## Communication

Official communication channels include:

- Website  
- Discord  
- Email  

Users are expected to stay updated through these platforms.

---

## Amendments

- Policies may be updated periodically  
- Changes are announced through official channels  
- Continued use of services implies acceptance of updates  

---

## Liability

VATSIM Pakistan is not responsible for:

- Misuse of simulation procedures  
- Incorrect interpretation of aviation practices  
- Real-world application of simulation content  

---

## Acknowledgement

By participating in VATSIM Pakistan services, users acknowledge that they:

- Understand this policy  
- Agree to comply with it  
- Accept governance under VATSIM Pakistan  

---

## Summary

This policy defines how VATSIM Pakistan operates as an organized subdivision, ensuring structured management, clear responsibilities, and alignment with global VATSIM standards.
