## Overview

This policy defines the structure, responsibilities, and operational rules for Air Traffic Controllers (ATC) within VATSIM Pakistan.

It ensures safe, consistent, and efficient air traffic control operations across Pakistani airspace.

---

## Purpose

The purpose of this policy is to:

- Standardize ATC operations within Pakistan vACC  
- Define controller responsibilities and limitations  
- Ensure compliance with VATSIM global policies  
- Maintain safe and realistic ATC services  

---

## General Provisions

- All controllers must comply with VATSIM global policies (Code of Conduct, CoR, GCAP)  
- Controllers must operate within their certified rating  
- Controllers must follow local procedures defined by Pakistan vACC  
- ATC services must remain professional and simulation-focused  

---

## Position Classifications

ATC positions within Pakistan are categorized into different levels based on complexity and responsibility.

### Tier 1 Positions

- Major international airports  
- High traffic volume  
- Require strong procedural knowledge  
- Typically includes:
  - OPKC (Karachi)
  - OPLA (Lahore)
  - OPIS (Islamabad)

---

### Tier 2 Positions

- Medium traffic airports  
- Less complex operations compared to Tier 1  
- Suitable for developing controllers  

---

### Unrestricted Positions

- Low traffic or uncontrolled environments  
- Suitable for beginner controllers  
- Minimal complexity  

---

## Controller Responsibilities

Controllers are expected to:

- Provide safe and orderly flow of traffic  
- Maintain separation between aircraft  
- Use correct phraseology  
- Follow published procedures  
- Coordinate with other ATC positions  

---

## Operational Rules

### Top-Down Control

- Higher positions cover lower positions if offline  
- Example:
  - CTR → APP → TWR → GND → DEL  

---

### Frequency Usage

- Only one primary frequency should be used  
- Additional frequencies only when required and authorized  
- Avoid frequency congestion  

---

### ATIS Requirements

ATIS must include:

- Airport identifier  
- Runway in use  
- Weather (QNH, wind, visibility)  
- Active procedures  

---

## Restrictions

Controllers must NOT:

- Control positions above their rating  
- Use incorrect or misleading information  
- Provide services outside authorized airspace  
- Ignore coordination procedures  

---

## Training & Authorization

- Controllers must complete required training before operating higher positions  
- Solo validations may be issued for specific positions  
- Continuous evaluation may be conducted  

---

## Enforcement

Failure to comply with this policy may result in:

- Removal from roster  
- Suspension of controlling privileges  
- Escalation to VATSIM authorities  

---

## Liability

VATSIM Pakistan is not responsible for:

- Misuse of procedures  
- Incorrect simulation practices  
- Any real-world aviation interpretation  

---

## Acknowledgement

By controlling within VATSIM Pakistan, users acknowledge and agree to:

- Follow this policy  
- Follow all VATSIM rules  
- Maintain professionalism at all times

## Summary

This policy ensures structured, safe, and standardized ATC operations within Pakistan vACC while maintaining alignment with VATSIM global standards.
