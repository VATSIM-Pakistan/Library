    Jinnah International Airport / OPKC

    General Information

    Jinnah International Airport (OPKC) is the busiest airport in Pakistan and serves as the primary gateway for both domestic and international traffic. It is located in Karachi and handles a mix of heavy international and regional operations.

    !!! note Expect stand allocation from ATC when online. Otherwise use realistic stand selection.

    Departure Procedures

    General

    • Expect standard IFR departures when ATC is online
    • Maintain assigned heading/altitude until cleared otherwise
    • Follow SID if issued

    Runway Usage

    Runway Typical Use
    25L / 25R Preferred for departures
    07L / 07R Used depending on winds

    Notes

    • Heavy traffic may require sequencing delays
    • Maintain separation after departure
    • Monitor assigned frequency immediately after takeoff

    Arrival Procedures

    General

    • Expect radar vectors for ILS approach
    • Descend via ATC instructions unless cleared via STAR
    • Maintain speed control when instructed

    Common Approaches

    Runway Type
    25L ILS
    25R ILS
    07L ILS
    07R ILS

    Notes

    • Expect sequencing during busy events
    • Maintain assigned speed/altitude
    • Report established on localizer if requested

    Taxi Procedures

    • Follow ATC instructions strictly when online
    • Use standard taxi routes when offline
    • Hold short of active runway unless cleared

    !!! warning Runway crossings require explicit clearance from ATC.

    Pilot Notes

    • Always check charts before flight
    • Monitor ATIS when available
    • Be prepared for vectors instead of full procedures
    • Use correct phraseology

    Controller Notes

    • Maintain separation between parallel runway ops
    • Coordinate GND / TWR / APP carefully
    • Sequence arrivals efficiently during events
    • Manage mixed traffic (heavy + domestic)
